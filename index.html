About

Bringing characters to life.

Mickey Farmer is an actor based in Oklahoma City, OK. Film training at OFA Studios (Oklahoma City); theater at Booker T. Washington High School for the Performing and Visual Arts; voice acting with Double AA Media (Aaron Michael) and Real Voice LA.

A fearless approach to character—Mickey goes there. Loves ad-lib, comedy, horror, and drama, and brings that same commitment to every set and every collaboration.
